## üé® Colors

### Primary Colors
- **Primary (CTA):** `#ff00ff` (Magenta)
- **Secondary:** `#00ff55` (Bright Green)
- **Tertiary:** `#00c3ff` (Cyan)

### Background Colors
- **Background:** `#070707` (Very Dark Grey/Black)
- **Cards:** `#111111` (Dark Grey)
- **Borders:** `#262626` (Medium Dark Grey)

### Text Colors
- **Primary Text:** `#fafafa` (Off-White)
- **Secondary Text:** `#a3a3a3` (Light Grey)

---

## üìù Typography

### Font Families
- **Headings/Buttons:** Space Mono (mono style)
- **Body:** Inter (clean sans)

### Font Sizes & Line Heights
- **H1:** `48px / 1.1`
- **H2:** `32px / 1.2`
- **H3:** `24px / 1.3`
- **Card:** `20px / 1.4`
- **Body:** `16px / 1.5`
- **Small:** `14px / 1.5`

### Typography Rules
- Keep body lines 60-80 characters
- Use Space Mono for headings and buttons
- Use Inter for body text
- If fonts unavailable, use clean mono + clean sans substitute

---

## ‚ú® Effects

### Neon Glow
- **Usage:** 1-2 elements per layout (hero + CTA)
- **Rule:** Neon is a spark, not the whole fire
- Apply subtle glow effects, not overwhelming

### Glitch Text
- **Usage:** Hero / special announcements only
- Use sparingly for maximum impact

### Layout Principles
- Keep whitespace generous
- Avoid clutter
- One clear CTA per section
- Keep hierarchy obvious

---

## üéØ Logo Guidelines

- Use approved logo files only
- **Minimum Size:** 
  - Symbol: 24px
  - Full lockup: 32px
- **Clear Space:** Padding = height of "b"
- **Never:** Recolor, stretch, rotate, add extra effects

---

## üì∏ Imagery

### Style
- Candid, real moments
- Diverse creators
- Tech-in-action: screens, building, collaboration

### Avoid
- Corporate stock vibes

### Overlays
- Subtle grids/particles/code
- Use gradients for legibility

---

## üí¨ Voice & Tone

**Tone:** Conversational, welcoming, energized

**Example:** "Never coded before? Perfect. We'll build it together."

### Voice Principles
- Be conversational, not corporate
- Welcome newcomers warmly
- Maintain energetic, positive energy
- Keep it accessible and inclusive
